Our Story

Founded in 2022, Calmic Sdn Bhd began as a specialized vessel cleaning service provider for the oil and gas industry. Today, we have evolved into a comprehensive technology solutions company, serving diverse clients with exceptional services and innovative products.

Our expertise spans engineering consultancy, remote surveillance and inspection services, industrial cybersecurity, and telecommunication engineering. At Calmic, we are driven by precision, safety, and innovation — making us strategic partners in building secure, connected, and efficient operations.

PhotoVault represents our commitment to providing cutting-edge digital solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients in document management and secure photo storage.
